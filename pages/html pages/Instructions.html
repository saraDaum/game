So before you start, Some instructions:


You need to find out which four colors your computer has selected from six colors.
Note: Do not choose one color twice!
Once you have selected the colors and clicked 'Continue',
you will see the four colors you have selected and the score on them.
A white circle shows you the right color that is also in the right position
while a red circle shows the right color that is not in the right place.
For your convenience, the number of guesses is not limited.
